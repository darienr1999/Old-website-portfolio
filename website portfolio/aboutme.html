About Me:

Hey there!

My name is Dairen Rogers and I am a Student at the Georgia Institute of Technology. My goal is to learn all the skills neccessary to become a great game designer in order to transform my ideas into new experiences for players around the world.

Where I come from

I was born in New Jersey and lived in a small town by the name of Basking Ridge for the majority of my youth. During the summer of 2015, me and my family moved way down south to Alpharetta GA, where I continued my education at Milton High School and graduated in 2018.

Why Game Design?

Video games have always been apart of my life. Every new video I played was an experience like no other. The feeling of diving into a digital worlds with engaging stories and fun gameplay mechanics gives me dopamine rush like no other. If there is even a small chance that I can create an experience like that for someone else, I will do everything I can to make it happen. I believe that if I get my degree in Computational Media and become a game developer, I will be able to create those experiences for other people.
